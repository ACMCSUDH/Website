About

ACM is widely recognized as the premier membership organization for computing professionals, delivering resources that advance computing as a science and a profession; enable professional development; and promote policies and research that benefit society.

ACM hosts the computing industry's leading Digital Library, and serves its global members and the computing profession with journals and magazines, conferences, workshops, electronic forums, and Learning Center.

At California State University Dominguez Hills ACM is the campus's computer science organization. We are a support group of passionate students who want to engage in projects that will help us excel in our future careers. In our group we approach challenging technical prompts to better our problem solving skills. Thus, the club will function as a gathering place for all those interested in programming technologies and research. The club will promote individuals to collaborate with others in many software-related projects, based on individual interest.
